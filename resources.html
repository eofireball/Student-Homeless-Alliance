Where to Find Us

We do not currently have a physical office location. Feel free to use the resources listed on this page if you are a student in need. Be sure to also follow us on social media to be informed of upcoming events and news on the housing crisis going on at San Jose State University.

Homeless Resources Map

Partners Info and Contact Info:

SJSU Cares is there to provide assistance when you need it most. They provide resources and services for SJSU students facing any kind of financial crisis. If you’re having trouble paying for food, housing or other bills, come to them to get the help you need.
