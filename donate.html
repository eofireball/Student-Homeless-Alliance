Give Hope

The Student Homeless Alliance is a non profit student organization. Our efforts are focused on brining awareness to the sad reality some students face. Our organization is solely dependent on volunteers and donations from the public.

Your donations will be used in several ways. Some of the funds will be use in our awareness campaign which consists of flyers, communitiy events and rallies. Most of the funds will be used to provide short term housing for homeless students

Ways to Help

There are many ways you can help students in need. As mentioned above our organization is dependent on volunteers. You can make a difference by being part of community events and awareness campaigns. These are held at different locations. All information can be found in our resources page.

Donations are another way to help this cause. Student Homeless Alliance does not received funding from San Jose State University or any entity. Therefore, we rely on donations from the public and concerned citizens. Your donation will directly benefit homeless students by providing them with temporary shelter whenever possible.

If you would like to donate, to the right are several ways to do so.
